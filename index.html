Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student in department of automation, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), I obatained my B. Eng. degree in department of electronic engineering, Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU) in 2018.

My research interests focus on computer vsion, especially low-level vision tasks, advised by Feng Zhao and Zhiwei Xiong, and I also work closely with Xueyang Fu. I supervise the low-level vision group of BIVLab.
